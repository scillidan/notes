### Obsidian

```
A-S-e     | \                   | Toggle Live Preview/Source mode
C-S-Down  | \                   | Split down
C-S-Right | \                   | Split right
C-S-p     | Command palette     | Open command palette
A-S-b     | BRAT                | All Commands list
C-f       | Omnisearch          | In-file search
C-S-f     | Omnisearch          | Vault search
C-Up      | Outliner            | Fold the list
C-Down    | Outliner            | Unfold the list
A-Up      | Scroll to Top       | Scroll to Top
A-Down    | Scroll to Top       | Scroll to Bottom
A-c       | Scroll to Top       | Scroll to Cursor
A-S-h     | Homepage            | Open homepage
```
