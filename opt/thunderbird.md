### [Thunderbird](https://www.thunderbird.net/)

#### Usage

````{tab} Outlook email
1. [Outlook](https://outlook.live.com) email → Settings → Mail → Forwarding and IMAP → POP and IMAP
2. Enable `POP`, `IMAP`
````

````{tab} Yandex email
1. [yandex](https://mail.yandex.com) → Settings → All settings → Email clients
2. Enable `IMAP`, `POP`
````
